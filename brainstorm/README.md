## One-paragraph pitch

Hiring is shifting. Engineers who are 10× with AI are not the same people who were 10× without it, and static whiteboard interviews are measuring the wrong thing. **Hirewave** is a two-sided AI-native hiring platform that evaluates how candidates *collaborate* with AI — not just whether they can write code from memory. Candidates solve real, multi-step problems in a sandboxed environment alongside an AI assistant. Employers get a ranked, bias-audited shortlist scored across 8 behavioral dimensions that actually predict on-the-job performance.

## Inspiration

- **hire.vizuara.ai (ArcEval)** — the closest reference. Sandboxed terminal, AI pair, 8-dimension scoring, side-by-side candidate comparisons. Hirewave differs by leading with a **two-sided landing** (we speak to employers and candidates with equal weight), a 3D-first visual language, and a broader role taxonomy (not just engineering).
- **21st.dev** — component library and magic-UI patterns. We don't install their runtime; we replicate the component aesthetics (Spotlight, BorderBeam, Meteors, Marquee, BentoGrid, Ripple, AuroraBackground, AnimatedGradientText, NumberTicker) directly in the repo so we own the primitives.
- **Linear / Vercel / Arc** — restraint, precision, density without noise.
