# Product thesis

## The shift

For a decade, hiring evaluated **raw production skill** — can you write the sort function, can you design the cache, can you debug from memory. In a world where every working engineer has an AI assistant, that rubric is obsolete. The signal that now predicts performance is **AI-collaboration literacy**: how well you break problems down, prompt precisely, verify output, detect hallucination, iterate, and know when to override the model.

Hirewave is the first hiring platform built for that signal.

## Users

**Candidates.** Early-career to senior. Frustrated by whiteboard interviews that don't reflect real work. Willing to do a 45–90 minute async assessment that actually shows what they can do.

**Employers.** Hiring managers and recruiters at AI-forward companies. Tired of screening hundreds of applicants with no meaningful signal. Want a ranked shortlist with evidence.

## Jobs to be done

For candidates:
- "Show me proving I'm good takes less than 2 hours and is interesting."
- "Give me feedback, even if I don't get the role."
- "Don't make me re-do the same assessment for every company."

For employers:
- "Filter 500 applicants down to 15 I should actually talk to."
- "Give me evidence, not a score out of 100."
- "Make it defensible — auditable, bias-checked, role-calibrated."

## The 8 dimensions

Every assessment scores a candidate across:

1. **Problem decomposition** — breaks fuzzy asks into sub-problems.
2. **Prompt precision** — gets useful output from the AI on the first try.
3. **Verification instinct** — tests, doesn't trust.
4. **Hallucination detection** — catches when the model is wrong.
5. **Iteration velocity** — learns from each attempt.
6. **Context management** — feeds the AI what it needs, withholds what it doesn't.
7. **Override judgment** — knows when to ignore the AI.
8. **Communication** — explains tradeoffs to a human reviewer.

These are the spec for both the scoring rubric and the shape of the assessment challenges.

## Differentiation vs. ArcEval

| | ArcEval | Hirewave |
|---|---|---|
| Positioning | Employer-led | Two-sided |
| Scope | Engineering | Engineering + design + ops |
| Portability | Per-company | Universal assessment passport |
| Feedback to candidate | Minimal | Full breakdown on every dimension |
| Visual identity | Flat | 3D / motion-first |
